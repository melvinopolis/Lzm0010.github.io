About

Bio

My name is Lee McCormick. I was born in Houston, Texas on May 21, 1990. At a young age, I developed a knack for being stubborn and refusing to quit on things even when I was terrible at them. Wanting to explore the world outside of Texas, I attended Auburn University and graduated with my degree in Business Administration in May of 2012. Shortly after, I received my MBA from the University of Alabama at Birmingham in December of 2013, while working full time in sales at Talladega Superspeedway and then part time at Atherotech sorting coolers. After receiving my MBA, I moved back to Houston to work with my family at a logistics company called Pinch Transport. I found a niche in accounting and was able to touch every aspect of the business, but struggled to find any passion in what I was doing. I would dream up several ideas without the ability to implement them. Meanwhile, my love for technology continued to grow as I would fantasize about the possibilities of making any process better. Finally in May of 2015, I gained the courage to leave a safe and complacent environment to pursue my dreams of making really cool things through the use of technology. To be continued...
